Before and After

Petaluma, Sonoma County, and California

Chris and Josh live in gorgeous Petaluma, a sickeningly quaint dairy town, around an hour north of the Golden Gate Bridge. Petaluma was founded in the 1850s, and is home to stunning Californian Victorian-era architecture which survived the 1906 and 1989 earthquakes that were catastrophic in the rest of the Bay Area. Petaluma is surrounded by hills, and defined by a river that flows into San Pablo Bay.

Petaluma is the southern-most city in Sonoma County, a mixed rural-suburban area, home to stunning nature reserves, much of the Californian craft brewing industry (Petaluma is home to Lagunitas), and also the largest region of Californian Wine Country (the Petaluma Gap wine region starts at the city limits). If you end up staying in or around Petaluma before or after the wedding, we’ll be sure to point you at the best that our county has to offer.
